## THM ROOM CIA triad

# What is this room about:

- This room explains the pinnacles of cybersecurity

- Explains the purpose of Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability

# What i learned:

- What is :
  - Confidentiality(Ensuring digital information is not available to unauthorized users)
  - Integrity(Ensuring digital information is not modified without permission)
  - Availability(Ensuring digital information is not unavailable when needed)
  
- I learned what scenarios may include for example confidentiality etc.

- I learned what is security mindset(way of instinctual thinking how systems can be attacked)

# Tasks :
