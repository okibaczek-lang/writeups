## THM ROOM Inside a Computer System

# What is this module about:

- The room explains how a computer is built, what components are inside the computer and how are they working

- What happens when you click the power button on your computer

# What i learned:

- What components builds a computer: Mother Board, CPU, RAM, SSD+HDD, GPU, PSU and how they works

- What happens when you boot your computer: 
Power Button – signal sent to PSU, power flows to components
UEFI/BIOS – firmware initializes hardware components
POST – tests presence and functionality of required components
Boot Device Selection – UEFI scans the priority list for a bootloader
Bootloader – loads the OS from storage into RAM and hands over control
